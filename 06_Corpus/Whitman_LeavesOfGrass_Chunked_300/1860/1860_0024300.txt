22 We thought our Union grand, and our Constitution grand, 
I do not say they are not grand and good, 
for they are, I am this day just as much 
in love with them as you, Then I am in 
love with you, and with all my fellows upon the 
earth. 23 We consider bibles and religions divine—I do not 
say they are not divine, I say they have all 
grown out of you, and may grow out of you 
still, It is not they who give the life—it is 
you who give the life, Leaves are not more shed 
from the trees, or trees from the earth, than they 
are shed out of you. 24 The sum of all
