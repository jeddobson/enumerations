less the reminder of property or qualities, and more the 
reminder of life, And go on the square for my 
own sake and for others' sakes, And make short account

fugitives, and them that plot and conspire. 136 Walt Whitman, 
an American, one of the roughs, a kosmos, Disorderly, fleshy, 
sensual, eating, drinking, breeding,No sentimentalist—no stander above men and wo- 
men, or apart from them, No more modest than immodest.137
