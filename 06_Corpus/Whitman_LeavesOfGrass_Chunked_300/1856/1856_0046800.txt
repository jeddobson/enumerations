316 30 — Poem of The Propositions of Nakedness. RESPONDEZ! 
Respondez!Let every one answer! Let all who sleep be waked! 
Let none evade—not you, any more than others!Let that which 
stood in front go behind! and let that which was 
behind advance to the front and speak!Let murderers, thieves, tyrants, 
bigots, unclean persons, offer new propositions!Let the old propositions be 
postponed! Let faces and theories be turned inside out! Let 
meanings be criminal as well as results! (Say! can results 
be criminal, and meanings not criminal?)Let there be no suggestion 
besides the suggestion of drudgery!Let none be pointed toward his 
destination! (Say! do you know your destination?)Let trillions of men 
and women be mocked with bodies and mocked with souls!Let 
the love that waits in them, wait! Let it die,
