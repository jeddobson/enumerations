escape utterly from others' anchors and holds! To drive free! 
to love free! to dash reckless and dangerous! To court 
destruction with taunts, with invitations! To ascend, to leap to 
the heavens of the love indicated to me! To rise 
thither with my inebriate soul! To be lost if it 
must be so! To feed the remainder of life with 
one hour of fulness and freedom! With one brief hour 
of madness and joy. OUT OF THE ROLLING OCEAN THE
