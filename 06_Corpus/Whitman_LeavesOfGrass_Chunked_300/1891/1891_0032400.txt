Walt Whitman? Who are they you salute, and that one 
after another salute you? I see a great round wonder 
rolling through space, I see diminute farms, hamlets, ruins, graveyards, 
jails, factories, palaces, hovels, huts of barbarians, tents of nomads 
upon the surface, I see the shaded part on one 
side where the sleepers are sleeping, and the sunlit part 
on the other side, I see the curious rapid change 
of the light and shade, I see distant lands, as 
real and near to the inhabitants of them as my 
land is to me. I see plenteous waters, I see 
mountain peaks, I see the sierras of Andes where they 
range, I see plainly the Himalayas, Chian Shahs, Altays, Ghauts, 
I see the giant pinnacles of Elbruz, Kazbek, Bazardjusi, I 
see the Styrian Alps, and the Karnac Alps, I see 
the Pyrenees, Balks, Carpathians, and to the north the Dofrafields, 
and off at sea mount Hecla, I see Vesuvius and 
Etna, the mountains of the Moon, and the Red mountains 
of Madagascar, I see the Lybian, Arabian, and Asiatic deserts,
