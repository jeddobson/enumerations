HER DEAD GAZING.PENSIVE on her dead gazing I heard the 
Mother of All, Desperate on the torn bodies, on the 
forms covering the battle- fields gazing, (As the last gun 
ceased, but the scent of the powder-smoke linger'd,) As she 
call'd to her earth with mournful voice while she stalk'd, 
Absorb them well O my earth, she cried, I charge 
you lose not my sons, lose not an atom, And
