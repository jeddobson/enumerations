am Walt Whitman, liberal and lusty as Nature, Not till 
the sun excludes you do I exclude you,Not till the 
waters refuse to glisten for you and the leaves to 
rustle for you, do my words refuse to glisten and 
rustle for you. My girl I appoint with you an 
appointment, and I charge you that you make preparation to 
be worthy to meet me, And I charge you that 
you be patient and perfect till I come.Till then I 
salute you with a significant look that you do not 
forget me. I WAS LOOKING A LONG WHILE.I WAS looking
