O SOUL.THIS day, O soul, I give you a wondrous 
mirror;Long in the dark, in tarnish and cloud it lay—But 
the cloud has pass'd, and the tarnish gone; …Behold, O 
soul! it is now a clean and bright mirror,Faithfully showing
